About ENZ Group

We are a leading licensed restoration contracting company, serving Pennsylvania and New Jersey. With extensive experience working alongside the claims departments of all major insurance companies, our team brings over 100,000 combined hours of expertise in home repair and construction.

As fully certified professionals, we handle everything from large, complex projects to smaller jobs with the same dedication. Driven by our commitment to excellence, we go above and beyond to ensure our clients are completely satisfied. Whether your home has sustained damage or you're planning a new construction, we’re here to assist. Contact us now for a free, no-obligation consultation, or fill out our contact form and we’ll reach out at our earliest convenience.
